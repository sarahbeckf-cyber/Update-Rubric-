How to use this tool

Enter the leader and the academic department, then select one or more cost centers under their scope. Visit volume and clinicians supervised are auto-populated from FY25 actuals and weighted ×1.5 as the primary drivers. The remaining metrics are scored manually. Hover the ⓘ icon next to any metric for its definition.

Data note: Visit volume and clinician count auto-populate from FY25 actuals. FY26 figures are not used here because FY26 totals are not yet annualized. Clinician count is unique-billing-provider headcount, not cFTE.
